About.

"DoodieMan" is a meme-based cryptocurrency running on the Ethereum blockchain. Fueled by a dedicated community, it embraces humor and irreverence, taking inspiration from the popular character Doodieman. With a unique twist, DoodieMan incorporates a 100% burn mechanism, making it deflationary by design. This means that with each transaction, tokens are permanently removed from circulation, potentially increasing the value of remaining tokens over time. It's essential to recognize the speculative nature of meme-based coins and exercise caution while participating in such projects within the cryptocurrency space.
